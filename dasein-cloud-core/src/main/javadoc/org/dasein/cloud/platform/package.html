Provides access to a variety of Platform as a Service (PaaS) type offerings. What different people call PaaS is a controversial topic in itself. Dasein considers anything a PaaS that is any kind of abstraction on top of core virtual infrastructure. To interact with this service, you will want to first fetch the platform services via {@link org.dasein.cloud.platform.PlatformServices}. This will of coourse give you access to the different kinds of services bundled under the PaaS label.

Current PaaS services:

  1. {@link org.dasein.cloud.platform.CDNSupport}
  2. {@link org.dasein.cloud.platform.bigdata.DataWarehouseSupport}
  3. {@link org.dasein.cloud.platform.KeyValueDatabaseSupport}
  4. {@link org.dasein.cloud.platform.MonitoringSupport}
  5. {@link org.dasein.cloud.platform.MQSupport}
  6. {@link org.dasein.cloud.platform.PushNotificationSupport}
  7. {@link org.dasein.cloud.platform.RelationalDatabaseSupport}
